NLP: Detection of Stress in Social Media

Stress is a universal human experience particularly in the online world. Too much stress is associated with many negative health outcomes, therefore making its identifaction useful across a range of domains. Identifying stressed people allows them to be targeted with helpful resources or de-stressing content. A staggering 70 million work days are lost each year due to mental health problems in the UK, costing employers approximately ¬£2.4 billion per year. This project called Dreaddit is a text corpus of lengthy multi-domain social media (Reddit) data for the identifaction of stress. The data set consists of 3.5k labelled segments by human annotators.
